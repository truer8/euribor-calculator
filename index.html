How it works

The calculator locks the Euribor rate on your loan start date and resets it every 3, 6, or 12 months based on your chosen tenor.

  • 3-Month Euribor: rate resets every 3 months
  • 6-Month Euribor: rate resets every 6 months
  • 12-Month Euribor: rate resets every 12 months

This calculator assumes a 0.00% Euribor floor based on common local bank practice. Negative Euribor values are treated as 0.00% and do not reduce the interest rate below the bank margin.

Start dates are limited from 2000-01 to the current month.

Data source: ECB / loaded JSON data
