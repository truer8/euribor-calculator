How it works

The calculator locks the Euribor rate on your loan start date and resets it every 3, 6, or 12 months based on your chosen tenor.

  • 3-Month Euribor: Rate resets every 3 months — more frequent adjustments
  • 6-Month Euribor: Rate resets every 6 months — balanced option
  • 12-Month Euribor: Rate resets every 12 months — less frequent adjustments

Data source: European Central Bank SDMX API
